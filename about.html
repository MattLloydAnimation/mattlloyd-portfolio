About me

I’m a freelance 2D animator and character designer focused on expressive, character-driven work. I have a background in motion graphics and also teach Illustration, Animation and Character Design for the University of the Arts London and the British Film Institute.

Past credits include the Bafta-shortlisted short film 'Your Mountain Is Waiting' and 'HAG', which premiered at SXSW 2026 and is currently touring festivals.

Past clients include music videos for bands including Metronomy, Tame Impala and the Arctic Monkeys as well as adverts for clients like Meta, Google and the BBC.

I’m currently based in the UK, but I’m open to remote work and collaborations from anywhere in the world and am also available for private tutoring.
